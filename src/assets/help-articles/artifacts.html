Artifacts

This setting determines whether the 12 Chozo Artifacts are randomly shuffled or not.

Vanilla

Artifacts are not shuffled, and kept in their vanilla game locations. They are as follows:

Randomized

Artifacts are shuffled after the upgrades and ammo expansions are shuffled.
